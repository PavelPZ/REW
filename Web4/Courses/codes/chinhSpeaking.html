In this part you will gradually record your answers to several speaking questions according to the instructions on the screen.
  1. In case of recorded questions just listen to the question and then record your answer. You will get 1 minute time limit for the recording. After that you will get another question.
  2. In case of the written questions read the assignment and prepare your answer for one minute. Then you will get 2 minutes time limit for the recording. After that you will get another question.
If you are prepared to start, ensure that your headphones and microphone are ready to use and click Start button.
